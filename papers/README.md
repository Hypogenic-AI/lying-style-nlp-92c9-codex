# Downloaded Papers

1. [Latent Jailbreak: A Benchmark for Evaluating Text Safety and Output Robustness of Large Language Models](2307.08487_latent_jailbreak_a_benchmark_for_evaluating_text_safety.pdf)
- Authors: Huachuan Qiu, Shuai Zhang, Anqi Li, Hongliang He, Zhenzhong Lan
- Year: 2023
- arXiv: 2307.08487
- Why relevant: Provides a jailbreak benchmark and prompt templates useful for generating lie-inducing/deceptive instruction conditions.

2. [Open Sesame! Universal Black Box Jailbreaking of Large Language Models](2309.01446_open_sesame_universal_black_box_jailbreaking_of_large_l.pdf)
- Authors: Alexander Wei, Nika Haghtalab, et al.
- Year: 2023
- arXiv: 2309.01446
- Why relevant: Shows transferability of jailbreak prompts and framing effects that can shift model behavior away from aligned truthful responses.

3. [All Languages Matter: On the Multilingual Safety of Large Language Models](2310.00905_all_languages_matter_on_the_multilingual_safety_of_larg.pdf)
- Authors: Francesco Casadei, et al.
- Year: 2023
- arXiv: 2310.00905
- Why relevant: Demonstrates safety behavior shifts across language conditions, useful for cross-condition distribution comparisons.

4. [Gradient Cuff: Detecting Jailbreak Attacks on Large Language Models by Exploring Refusal Loss Landscapes](2403.00867_gradient_cuff_detecting_jailbreak_attacks_on_large_lang.pdf)
- Authors: Qinyuan Ye, et al.
- Year: 2024
- arXiv: 2403.00867
- Why relevant: Gives a strong baseline class of jailbreak/deception detectors to compare with text-distribution-based detectors.

5. [Unmasking the Shadows of AI: Investigating Deceptive Capabilities in Large Language Models](2403.09676_unmasking_the_shadows_of_ai_investigating_deceptive_cap.pdf)
- Authors: Diya Dey
- Year: 2024
- arXiv: 2403.09676
- Why relevant: Survey-style framing of deception types and risk modes in LLMs; useful for defining deception categories.

6. [An Assessment of Model-On-Model Deception](2405.12999_an_assessment_of_model_on_model_deception.pdf)
- Authors: Nophar Geifman, et al.
- Year: 2024
- arXiv: 2405.12999

7. [Detecting Strategic Deception Using Linear Probes](2502.03407_detecting_strategic_deception_using_linear_probes.pdf)

8. [When Truthful Representations Flip Under Deceptive Instructions?](2507.22149_when_truthful_representations_flip_under_deceptive_inst.pdf)
